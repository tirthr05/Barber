Our Mission

At Picasso2 Men's BarberShop, we believe that true beauty lies in giving. With this in mind, we decided to be a part of hope for every child battling illness by donating 10% of our haircut fee to support CHEO Children's Hospital, which supports cancer patients and children with disabilities. By visiting us, your contribution is not limited to a stylish look, but extends to a touch of kindness that brightens the lives of children in need. Together, we make a difference and bring smiles to the faces of those who deserve life.
